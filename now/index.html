Complete Beginner on Try Hack Me#

Having completed both The Cyber Mentor’s Linux 101 and Ropper’s Computing Fundamentals courses I’m now following the Complete Beginner path on try Hack Me.

Fire and Bones#

Been seeing a lot of traumas lately while on duty at the firehouse. Which means there’s a lot of sights, images, sounds, and smells flashing across my mental movie screen. Have some time-off lined up so I’ll head out of town for a spell. Some place with a kickin’ local music scene.

School’s in Session#

Spring semester at the college has begun and I’m once again teaching emergency medicine. The winter break always feels too short but it’s always cool to get back into the class and see a new generation coming up.

Reading …the World Ends#

Well written and engaging, This Is How They Tell Me the World Ends: The Cyberweapons Arms Race has been amazing so far. I’m about 25% into it and wish I had more time to devote to simply reading it more.

Listening Militant Vinylist#

Recently came across this dope-ass channel of old-school hip-hop mixes. Throw on the Wu-Tang or Soul Assassins mix and let’s go 12 rounds on that heavy bag.

Watching Only Murders in the Building#

Good gods this show is enjoyable! The only thing I’d heard was that it stars my two favorite Martins - Steve and Short - and knew I had to tune in. They (along with Selena Gomez) start a podcast to investigate a murder in the building. The characters and story are nuanced, likable, and a lot of fun.
